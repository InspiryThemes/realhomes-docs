If you are using **RealHomes Vacation Rentals**, a **Vacation Rentals** tab will appear at the top. Add pricing, availability, and other details here.

---

### **Dual Pricing (Sale and Rent)**

Starting from **RealHomes v4.4.5**, the theme supports **Dual Pricing**.

To enable this feature, navigate based on your version:

=== "v4.5.1 and Later"

    !!! success "RealHomes Settings"
        <span class="nav-step">Dashboard</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">RealHomes</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Settings</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Property Settings</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Units</span>

    ![Enable Dual Pricing](images/add-content/dual-property-price-settings.png)

=== "v4.5.0 and Earlier"

    !!! info "Legacy Settings"
        <span class="nav-step">Dashboard</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Easy Real Estate</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Settings</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Price Format</span>

    Enable the **Dual Property Price** option.

    ![Enable Dual Pricing](images/add-content/enable-dual-property-price.png)

Once enabled, you can add both **sale** and **rent prices** on the property edit screen.

![Vacation Rentals](images/add-content/add-property-vr.png)

### **iCalendar Synchronization**

Stay updated and avoid double bookings by syncing your calendar.

##### **Step 1. Add iCalendar Feed Page**
Create a page using the **iCalendar Feed** template.

![Property Basic Information](images/add-content/add-icalendar-page.png)

##### **Step 2. Syncing to third-party calendars**
Copy the **Feed URL** from the bottom of the *Vacation Rentals* property metabox and add it to the third-party site.

![iCalendar import & export](images/add-content/icalendar-import-export.png)

##### **Step 3. Syncing from third-party calendars**
Add third-party feed URLs to the *iCalendar Import* section in the metabox.

![iCalendar import & export](images/add-content/icalendar-import-export.png)
