# Logo and Tagline Settings

To configure footer logo area settings, follow the navigation path based on your version of the **RealHomes** theme:

=== "v4.5.1 and Later"

    !!! success "RealHomes Settings"
        <span class="nav-step">Dashboard</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">RealHomes</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Settings</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Footer</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Logo</span>

    ![RealHomes Settings - Footer Logo](images/footer/footer-logo-ultra-settings.png)

=== "v4.5.0 and Earlier"

    !!! info "Legacy Settings"
        <span class="nav-step">Dashboard</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">RealHomes</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Customize Settings</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Footer</span> ➤ <span class="nav-step">Logo and Tagline</span>

    ![Legacy Customizer - Footer Logo Tagline Ultra](images/footer/footer-logo-tagline-ultra.png)
