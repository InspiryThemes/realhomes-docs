# **Add/Edit an Agency**

This guide explains how to add a new real estate agency to your website.

To get started, navigate to **Dashboard → Easy Real Estate → Agencies** and click the **Add New** button.

![Add New Agency](images/add-content/add-new-agency-button.png)

---

### **1. Agency Name and Description**

-   **Title:** Enter the official name of the agency.

-   **Description:** Use the content editor to write a detailed profile for the agency, such as its mission, history, and areas of expertise. This information will be displayed on the agency's public profile page.

![Agency Name and Description](images/add-content/add-agency-title-description.png)

---

### **2. Agency Logo (Featured Image)**

In the **Featured Image** panel on the right side, upload the agency's official logo.

![Set Agency Logo](images/add-content/agency-logo-image-gutenberg.png)

!!!tip "Image Guidelines"
    For best results, use a logo image that is at least **210px wide and 210px high**. Larger square images are recommended, as they will be automatically cropped to fit.

---

### **3. Agency Details**

Below the main content editor, you will find the **Provide Related Information** section. This section has multiple tabs for entering the agency's specific details.

#### **Basic Tab**

This tab contains settings for the agency's contact form and verification status.

-   **Shortcode to Replace Default Agency Form:** You can paste a shortcode from a plugin like Contact Form 7 or WPForms here to use a custom contact form instead of the default one.

-   **Mark as Verified:** Setting this to "Yes" can display a "Verified" badge on the agency's profile, depending on your theme's settings.

![Agency Basic Information](images/add-content/agency-information.png)

#### **Contact Tab**

Switch to the **Contact** tab to provide the agency's public contact information. This includes phone numbers, website, email address, and physical address. The email address provided here will receive messages sent through the agency's contact form.

![Agency Contact Information](images/add-content/agency-contact-information.png)

#### **Social Tab**

Use the **Social** tab to add direct links to the agency's social media profiles. These will be displayed as clickable icons on their profile page.

![Agency Social Media Links](images/add-content/agency-social-information.png)

---

### **5. Publish**

Once you have filled in all the necessary information, click the **Publish** button to make the agency's profile live on your website.
